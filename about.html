Common Exercises

The following are common exercise that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily exercise routine.

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full body exercise to increase your strength and endurace. Begin in a standing position, drip into a squat and extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and then forward again quickly, and then jump up form a squatted position.
Plank
Planks build your core strength. To perform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Moutain Climber
Moutain climbers are a good cardio exercise. Place your hand on the floor in a push up position, then bring one knee up to your cchest and then swtch as quickly as you can (as though you are climbing a moutain).
